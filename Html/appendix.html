How to fill out the form correctly.

Complete each section of the form as recommended in the table below.
"Full name" The name must be written in the same way as it is written in your identity document. A document is required.
"Mark the right statements." In this section, you need to mark the statements that correspond to your status.
"Gender" In this section, you need to select your actual gender.
"Regions of stay for the last year: " In this section, you need to select one or more camps in which you stayed for one or more weeks during the last year. No document needed.
"Country of birth" In this section, you need to select the country of your birth. Must match the information from the identity document. A document is required.
"Briefly tell us about yourself." In this section, you need to write what you think is important. No document needed.
